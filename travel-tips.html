Hotels

The wedding is at La Compañía which is beautiful but pricey, with rooms around $450/night. If you're okay with the cost for the full experience, we recommend it. However, there are more affordable hotels nearby that offer better value. We're staying at La Compañía for three nights before moving down the street to Sofitel... shhhh!

Here is a list of recomendations other than La Compañía in descending order by price.

**Note: if you stay in Casco Viejo, everything is walking distance to La Compañía.

Sofitel

~$400/night

While not much less per night, your money goes a lot farther at Sofitel if you want a luxury experience. They offer an included breakfast option which is great! Ask for a city view.
